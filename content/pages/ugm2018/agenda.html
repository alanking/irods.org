Food Truck Rodeo


Tuesday, June 5 | 5:00 p.m. - 7:30 p.m.
Durham Convention Center | 301 W. Morgan Street, Durham, NC

Whether you've wrapped up your day of iRODS training or have just arrived in town for UGM on Wednesday and Thursday, please join us outside the Durham Convention Center for food and fun!

Chirba Chirba and Cousins Maine Lobster will be providing their full menu for our enjoyment, and there will be corn hole, a life-size chess set, and music. Special thanks to RENCI for sponsoring this event!

Be sure to wear your UGM event bracelet to help vendors provide you with prompt service.
